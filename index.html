Biology finally becomes a real science.

For over half a century, biology generated massive amounts of data — and almost no certainty.

Pathways multiplied. Mechanisms contradicted one another. Diseases remained poorly understood.

This was not because life is mysterious.

It was because biology lacked rules.

What went wrong

Modern biology assumed that accumulating enough data would eventually produce understanding. That assumption failed.

  • Contradictions became acceptable
  • Context replaced causality
  • Memorization replaced reasoning
  • Prediction became impossible

Biology became a descriptive science — not an explanatory one.

What Rule-Based Biology is

Rule-Based Biology™ is a complete, law-driven framework of life.

It is not an improvement of biochemistry or molecular biology. It is their replacement.

The rules define:

  • How molecular functional groups behave
  • How conformations change dynamically
  • How structure, location, and energy determine function
  • How biological processes arise lawfully

Once the rules are known, the mechanisms follow automatically.

Why this works

This framework does not depend on new experiments.

It reorganizes what already exists:

  • Decades of published research
  • Thousands of drug studies
  • Millions of experimental observations

When the rules are applied, contradictions collapse and biology becomes coherent.

Authority does not decide truth.
