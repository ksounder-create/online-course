What This Site Is

Rule-Based Biology is a reconstruction of biological science based on the discovery of governing rules that determine how biological systems operate.

It is not a theory, not a model, and not a reinterpretation of existing biology. It is a framework built from identifying rules implicit in experimental data.

Why This Site Exists

Modern biology accumulated enormous amounts of data but failed to converge on a coherent explanation of life.

This occurred because biology developed without a validated rule system. Without rules, data remains descriptive and often contradictory.

The purpose of this site is to present the consequences of discovering those missing rules.

The Central Insight

Rules give data meaning.

Once rules are known:

  • Biological behavior becomes predictable
  • Contradictions disappear
  • Mechanisms become clear
  • Understanding replaces memorization
